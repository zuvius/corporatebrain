# BRAND NAME ALTERNATIVES
## Corporate Brain Rebrand Options

**Document Date:** April 2026  
**Current Name:** Corporate Brain  
**Naming Challenge:** Current name sounds like an internal project, not a market-ready product. Too descriptive, lacks distinctiveness, difficult to trademark.

---

## NAMING CRITERIA

| Criterion | Weight | Why It Matters |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| **Availability** | Critical | Must have .com, .ai, or strong .io available |
| **Memorability** | High | Easy to spell, say, remember after one mention |
| **Distinctiveness** | High | Not generic, trademarkable, stands out in market |
| **Relevance** | Medium | Suggests knowledge/intelligence without being literal |
| **International** | Medium | Works across languages, no negative connotations |
| **Length** | Low | Prefer 4-8 letters, max 3 syllables |
